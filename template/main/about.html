About Mahaan Exports

Mahaan Exports is a family-operated agricultural export house providing premium oyster mushrooms, organic herbs, specialty spices and fresh farm produce to international buyers. Our operations combine time-tested cultivation with modern post-harvest handling to deliver traceable, export-ready produce with consistent quality.

Company Introduction

Founded to bridge smallholder quality farms with global markets, Mahaan Exports focuses on sustainable cultivation, hygienic processing and export-compliant packing. We partner with chefs, retailers and distributors to supply seasonal and year-round crops with dependable lead times.

Capabilities

  • HACCP-ready packing & cold-chain workflows
  • Bulk & retail packaging (clamshells, MAP, kraft pouches, jars)
  • Custom private-label options and B2B programs
  • Sample management & export documentation handling
