Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Mahaan Exports (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you interact with our website, services, and enquiry forms. By using our website, you agree to the terms stated below.

We may collect the following information from users:

  • Personal Information: Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Company Name (if provided), and any message submitted via enquiry/contact forms.

Your information is used for:

  • Responding to enquiries and customer support

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. While we strive for maximum security, no digital transmission is 100% secure.
